WHAT IS TIDE

Founded in 2023, TIDE initially started out as an unnamed group of like-minded individuals formed during the development of the founding members' Capstone Project during their senior year in college. After successfully completing their Capstone Project and graduating from the university, the group eventually reconnected and set out to make a new wave in the name of web development and tech education in the country.
